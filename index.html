Recommendations for the management of depression in adults with epilepsy: an expert consensus*

Study design

A qualitative study conducted in 4 steps:

  1. Development of a questionnaire on the management of depression in PWE to be answered;
  2. Literature review and drafting initial recommendations;
  3. A nominal group methodology for reviewing initial recommendations and formulating new recommendations on those issues without available evidence;
  4. Drafting and approving the final recommendations.
